Events We Help Everything

WHE has a two pronged approach to encourage sustainable development in NYC communities. Firstly, we encourage future passion and appreciation for community service through teen volunteer groups. Secondly, we partner with local organizations to raise money for a problem in their community. By interacting and engaging with members of these communities, we are able to sustain the impact of donations.
